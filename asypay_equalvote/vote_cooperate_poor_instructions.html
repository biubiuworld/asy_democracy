Vote Stage

In the vote stage, you need to decide which game you vote for. After all of you make decisions, which game is implemented depends on two rules:

  • Firstly, the vote result is based on the majority rule. That is, if three of you choose game A, then game A wins.
  • Secondly, your group decision is taken into consideration with a 50% chance. For example, if your group decides game A, but your group decision is not taken into account, the computer will randomly choose a game for you.
You will be informed about the voting results after you make your own vote decision and click the next button.
