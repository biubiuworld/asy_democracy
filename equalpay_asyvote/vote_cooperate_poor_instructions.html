Vote Stage

In the vote stage, you need to

  1. choose the amount of additional shares you want to buy;
  2. decide which game you vote for.
After all of you make decisions, which game is implemented depends on two rules:
  • Firstly, the vote result is based on the majority rule. That is, if over half of shares vote for game A, then game A wins.
  • Secondly, your group decision is taken into consideration with a 50% chance. For example, if your group decides game A, but your group decision is not taken into account, the computer will randomly choose a game for you. In that case, you don't need to pay for your additional shares.
You will be informed about the voting results after you make your own vote decision and click the next button.
