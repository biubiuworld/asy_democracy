Welcome to the experiment!

Please now turn off your cell phone and do not communicate with other participants. You will remain anonymous to each other in this experiment. After completing the experiment, you will fill out a survey.

During the experiment, your payment will be calculated in experimental currency (points). After the experiment, the total points you earned will be converted into dollars. The exchange rate is:

10 points = 1 dollar

You are guaranteed a show-up fee of $6.00. Additional payment is possible based on the points you earn in the games.
